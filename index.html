about the shipyard

There are several types of shipyards including :

-Building dock shipyard

Shipyards or shipyards that only carry out work on the process of building new ships including ship design work, installation of initial ivory, installation of hull plates, installation of equipment, checking by Quality Control, various kinds of function tests, to carrying out ship class acceptance surveys by ship classification bodies that have been appointed

-Repair dock shipyard

Shipyards or shipyards that only carry out ship repair and ship maintenance work. The work includes maintenance and repair of ship hull construction, main engine maintenance, propulsion system repair and so on

-Building and repair dock shipyard

Shipyards or shipyards that carry out the process of making new ships as well as maintenance and repair of old ships

Shipyards or shipyards in several regions of Indonesia use various methods to assist the process of launching new ships or the process of raising ships from waters to land. These methods include :

1.Slipway dock / dok tarik

2.Graving dock / dak gali atau dok kolam

3.Floating dock / dok apung

The shipyard or shipyard must be able to provide suitable equipment and facilities that allow material handling, construction processes and so on. The ship classification agency has the right to carry out inspections at shipyards or shipyards. Shipyards or shipyards must also have qualified personnel in their fields
